History

                Founded in 2002 by chefs Daniel and Rafael, the 
                Vancouver restaurant swiftly rose to prominence,
                earning a coveted Michelin star. Their culinary 
                collaboration showcased a fusion of local ingredients
                and global influences, resulting in an innovative and
                tantalizing menu. With their unwavering dedication to 
                quality and creative expertise, they established
                themselves as luminaries in Vancouver's dynamic 
                dining scene, delighting guests with an unforgettable 
                gastronomic experience.

Our Philosophy

            Welcome to our world of colorful cuisine! Each dish is a vibrant masterpiece, 
            bursting with flavors and hues. From fiery red curries to verdant green salads, 
            our menu is a kaleidoscope of taste and visual delight. Savor saffron-infused 
            rice adorned with ruby-red pomegranate jewels, or indulge in our golden 
            turmeric-spiced chickpea stew. Join us on this gastronomic journey where colors 
            and flavors unite to create a feast for your senses.
